Token overview

Token Utility

The MUR Token will be used within the Murmur application for three main use cases

  • Incentivize user actions
  • Maintain reputation scores
  • User payments for protocols built on Murmur

How to register for the airdrop

  • Download our Mobile app on Android starting from October 12th.
  • Register for a new account with Murmur.
  • Post at least 2 murmurs. These must be valid Murmurs, and not just hi/hello.
  • View your referral code and invite 3 of your contacts.
  • Join our Telegram chat; Liking our facebook page is optional.
  • Receive 100 MUR Tokens after registering on the app, and 20 MUR Tokens for each friend who registers with your referral code. Token distribution will start from October 30th. Tokens will be distributed to the account created on the app.
